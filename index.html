Crafted with love, enjoyed with joy

We offer a vibrant fusion of traditional Indian cuisine enriched with the distinctive flavors of the Himalayas. While our dishes remain true to their cultural roots, we take pride in sourcing most of our ingredients locally. Everything on our menu—from sauces and naans to desserts—is handcrafted in-house from scratch.

Nepal's culinary heritage is diverse, with many dishes deeply rooted in local tradition, while others draw inspiration from neighboring India and China. About Us

Welcome to Cumin Flavor, where culinary traditions meet modern innovation. Our restaurant brings together the rich flavors of Indian and Nepalese cuisine, creating a unique dining experience that celebrates the diverse culinary heritage of the Himalayas.

Our chefs are passionate about preserving authentic recipes while incorporating fresh, locally-sourced ingredients. Every dish is prepared with care, from our handcrafted sauces to our freshly baked naans.

We believe that great food brings people together, and we're committed to providing an atmosphere where friends and family can gather to enjoy exceptional cuisine and create lasting memories.
